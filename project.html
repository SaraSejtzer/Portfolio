Semester 1

Photoshop

You have to choose at least one photograph from the series of photographs taken on the excursion. The photograph/photographs don't have to be taken by you, but no photographs from the internet. The photograph/photographs shall be edited in Photoshop so they uphold the following requirements:

  • Remove an element.
  • Add an element.
  • Correct the lighting in the photograph.
  • Crop the photograph, etc rotate the image (vertical/horizontal).
  • The photograph has to be a 4:3 format and saved so it's smaller or equal 1 M pixel.
  • Upload the original and the edited version side by side.

The first project I did whilst studying multimedia design, was a Photoshop assignment. This is the before and after. I wanted to make a campaign about mental health, and encourage people to seek family, friends or professional help when "their mind gets stormy".
